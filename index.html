History Of The Nightcourt

In a foreign land, in the distant past, there was once a renowned institution known as the Court of Night-Blooming Flowers, or the Night Court. Spanning an entire mountainside, the Night Court was home to a number of Houses that catered to patrons seeking pleasure, pain, and beauty.

For one night only, the fabled Night Court will intersect with our world once again in the sprawling gardens of Lighthaven.

From the production team who brought you the prediction market dating show Bet on Love comes an immersive All Hallows' Eve party designed to tip you off the ledge of the mundane.

Wander through decorated rooms and perfumed gardens, sampling the skills of our adepts. Men and women trained in the arts of mystery and persona will guide you through sensual experiences, confounding games, humorous dialogues, and much more.

Attire & Aesthetic

Dress in attire that evokes mystery, fantasy, or refinement: gothic ballgowns, tuxedos, fallen angels, regal demons draped in gold, faeries adorned with gossamer and thorn.

Secrets & Mysteries

This is a night designed in equal measure for love and fear. However, not all is as it appears. There may be secrets for you to discover that take you deeper into the world—rare white flowers that bloom in the night, an old crone with a message of ruin, spies carrying secrets in their sleeves.

Inspired by the world of the fantasy novel Kushiel's Dart, ornamented with original Bay Area-relevant twists.
